Ketika Ibu di Rumah... , a book from Paramadina Women Institute

https://www.instagram.com/p/CMI_YYMgOkg/

Ketika Ibu di Rumah... is a book made by members of Paramadina Women Institute. This book tells about daily life, some tips, and meaningful stories that author experienced during COVID-19 pandemic and have to struggle to overcome any obstacles as a mom and lecturer (most authors are lecturer). Hopefully, this book could inspire many audiences / readers (especially moms) and motivate them to keep their spirit doing all activities
