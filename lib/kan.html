Komite Akreditasi Nasional social media content

https://www.instagram.com/kan_akreditasi/?hl=id

The National Accreditation Committee (KAN) wants to do social media activation with the #bangkitKAN campaign by creating content that contains education about accreditation, as well as assessment activities carried out by KAN. Every 3 months KAN will change the color of the Instagram design template, with a total work contract of 9 months and produce 3 different templates.
