Project report of electric steam power plant (PLTU) - PT Huadian Bukit Asam Power

https://www.hbap.co.id/

A Chinese company, China Huadian Corporation, LTD. and Indonesia public company PT Bukit Asam, Tbk. has a collaboration to build power plant infrastructure and decide to make joint-venture called PT Huadian Bukit Asam Power (PT HBAP).

Power plant built in Sumatra island started in 2012 and will be launched in 2021. In this project, PT HBAP need to verify their patented technology before use it on its power plant, to do that PT HBAP have a partnership with Ikatan Auditor Teknologi Indonesia (IATI) an Indonesian Technology Auditor Association.

They also want to publish this project in media to have a better image in public. So they have partnership with PT Pekan Raya Eksibisi Indonesia (Presindo) a marketing communication, event, and media company.
